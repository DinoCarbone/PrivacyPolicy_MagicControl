## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Dino Carbone** ("we", "us", or "the developer") handles information in connection with the mobile game **Magic Control** ("the Game"). By downloading, installing, or using the Game, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

The Game does **not** require you to create an account or log in. We do **not** ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other information that directly identifies you.

---

## Information Stored on Your Device

The Game saves your progress and settings locally on your device using Android **PlayerPrefs** (local key-value storage). This includes data such as game progress, scores, and in-game preferences.

This data:

- Stays on your device.
- Is **not** transmitted to us or to any server we control.
- Is removed when you uninstall the Game or clear its data.

---

## Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The Game integrates the following third-party software development kits (SDKs), which may collect and process certain data automatically:

- **AppMetrica** (analytics, provided by Yandex)
- **Facebook SDK** (analytics and attribution, provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.)

These services may collect or transmit the following data:

1. **Device or other identifiers** — device identifiers and advertising identifiers used to recognize a device.
2. **App activity** — in-app interactions, events, and how you use the Game.
3. **Other app performance data** — additional information about how the Game runs on your device.
4. **Diagnostics** — technical data such as performance and operational metrics.
5. **Crash logs** — error reports and crash data used to identify and fix problems.

> Under the Google Play Data safety taxonomy, items 3–5 fall under the broader category **App info and performance**.

This data is collected by and transmitted to the respective third parties to provide analytics, measure performance, and attribute installs. We do not sell this data.

For details on how these third parties process data, please review their policies:

- **AppMetrica / Yandex Privacy Policy:** https://yandex.com/legal/confidential/
- **Meta (Facebook) Privacy Policy:** https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/

The data collected through the SDKs above is used to:

- Analyze installs and aggregate usage statistics.

We do **not** use this data to personally identify you.
